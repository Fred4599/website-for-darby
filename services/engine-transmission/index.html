warning Signs of Engine or Transmission Trouble

  • Unusual knocking, ticking, or grinding noises from the engine
  • Transmission slipping, hard shifting, or delayed engagement
  • Oil spots or fluid leaks under your vehicle
  • Reduced fuel economy or loss of power
  • Check engine light or transmission warning light illuminated
